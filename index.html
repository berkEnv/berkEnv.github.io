Environmental Remediation & Habitat Restoration Services

Remediation Services

Berkeley Environmental offers environmental remediation and habitat restoration services for industrial, residential, agricultural, and coastal sites. For sites big and small, we are committed to designing and overseeing remediations projects which meet and exceed regulatory cleanup standards.

  • Enhanced In-Situ Bioremediation
  • Chemical Oxidation
  • Thermal Treatment
  • Surfactant Flushing
  • Jet Injection
  • Excavation

Restoration Services

We bring an environmental science perspective to the restoration of remediation project sites. At its core, our goal is to assist, support, and enable the recovery of areas that have been degraded, damaged, or destroyed. In addition, we strive to restore environments using native plant species. Our environmental restoration services include:

  • Wetland Restoration
  • Wildlife Habitat Restoration
  • River Geomorphology Restoration
  • Restoration of Groundwater and Surface Flow Interaction
  • Erosion Control, Nutrient Barriers, Dewatering, Sediment Dredging, etc.

Research + Innovation

One of the factors that distinguishes Berkeley Environmental is our focus on research and innovation in the remediation and restoration sectors.

In addition to employing traditional remediation methods, Berkeley Environmental works to develop and innovative new approaches and strategies for remediating soil and water contaminants in urban, rural, and coastal settings.

About

Berkeley Environmental is a US company with offices in California. Our core team has decades of experience with project design, management, evaluation, and research at sites in North America, Europe, Africa, Asia, and Latin America.
